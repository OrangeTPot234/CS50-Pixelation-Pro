Who really is Kobe Bryant?

Kobe Bryant was more than a player. He was a father, a storyteller, an inspiration, and a true example of what hard work looks like. At just 21 years old, he was able to win his first NBA championship. Despite having many failures such as his loss to the Boston Celtics in the championship game in 2008, he always found ways to inspire himself and keep working hard. Kobe Bryant was a fierce competitor and true friend. He even made his own sports academy to encourage young women to unleash their potential through sports.
