Lakers Superstar

Kobe Bryant was widely considered to be one of the greatest basketball players of all time. In his 20 year career, Bryant was a five-time NBA champion, 2x Finals Most Valuable Player (2009, 2010), and a 2x gold medalist (2008, 2012) with the USA basketball team. After retirement, Bryant went on to win the Oscar award for the best animated short film.
